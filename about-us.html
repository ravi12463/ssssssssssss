About Us

Float - social networking app is developed from Ziyou Technologies Pvt. Ltd., a Bengaluru based company, Powered by Artificial Intelligence technology, Float app is coined to provide entertainment to a wide range of people throughout India in respective regional languages. This app is a creative and newly developed platform to make its users staying connected in a positive and healthy online environment.

About the idea and vision of float

Float was founded by two young entrepreneurs – who pursued their masters in Business Management and came across a unique idea of connecting people of India in their own regional languages. The idea is started when they fortunately encountered a post in one of the global social media apps (Facebook) with translate option that has shown an inappropriate context no way near to the original post. This triggered these young minds to make a dedicated interactive app that allows its users to share and post content in their very own regional languages and help them engaging more into social media. The idea further enhanced to voice chat rooms, live streaming, and fun-filled gaming to keep this platform an all-in-one place where users can spend time in group voice chatting, finding new connections, showcasing talents and much more. Besides, they also corroborated that Float is the 1stmobile application from the country to introduce voice chat rooms as Make in India initiative.
